About

Welcome to Pokémon World - Your Ultimate Pokémon Companion! Whether you're a seasoned Trainer or just starting your journey, Pokémon World is your go-to source for exploring the vast world of Pokémon. Dive into detailed information on every Pokémon across all generations - including types, evolutions, abilities, stats, and more! Our mission is to make it easy and fun to discover, learn, and master everything about the Pokémon universe. From classic favorites to the latest additions, you'll find everything you need to build the perfect team, plan your strategies, or simply satisfy your curiosity.

  • Features:
  • Full Pokédex entries for every known Pokémon
  • Stats, abilities, and evolution paths
  • Region-specific data from Kanto to Paldea
  • Advanced search and filtering tools
  • Helpful guides for new and veteran Trainers
